pickupthephone.club

The Problem

Email and text don't convey tone, resulting in lost empathy, nuance, and occasional efficiency.

The Solution

Be more "phone-prone," demonstrating a willingness to pick up the phone and call your friend, customer, or mom (even if they texted or emailed you).

pickupthephone.club is a personal commitment to reply to more emails and texts with phone calls. It's that simple.
